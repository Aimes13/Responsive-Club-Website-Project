A place to find your rhythmn and to put your musical skills to good use. Whether you are a rookie or a novice, there is a drum for everyone.

"Turn a bang into a beat!"

Schedule

Our weekly sessions include practising different drumming techniques, learning basic rhythmn, reading and writing music sheets.

Workshops

Our workshops teach musicality, mindfulness and drum culture; which have been proven to improve social skills and confidence.

Therapeutic Drum Circles

We occasionally offer therapeutic drum circles and allow the music to demonstrate its healing power.

Performances

We provide performance opportunities at a wide range of events, and we also encourage our members to participate.
